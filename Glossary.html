Quick Reference Tables

Optimal Temperatures

  • Maize: 18-32°C
  • Beans: 15-25°C
  • Groundnut: 25-30°C
  • Rice: 20-35°C
  • Wheat: 10-24°C
  • Cassava: 25-29°C

Rainfall Requirements

  • Maize: 500-800mm
  • Beans: 300-500mm
  • Groundnut: 500-600mm
  • Rice: 1000-1500mm
  • Wheat: 450-650mm
  • Cassava: 1000-1500mm

Growing Seasons

  • Maize: 90-120 days
  • Beans: 70-90 days
  • Groundnut: 90-110 days
  • Rice: 110-150 days
  • Wheat: 120-150 days
  • Cassava: 8-24 months
