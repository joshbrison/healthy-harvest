Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated:

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how Healthy Harvest collects, uses, protects, and shares information when you visit or use our platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

When you register or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name - To personalize your experience
  • Email address - For account verification and communication
  • Phone number - For SMS notifications (when voluntarily provided)
  • Location data - To provide localized weather and market information

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Interaction with features and services

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve user experience and website performance
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services - Disease diagnosis, weather forecasts, market prices
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests - Help with technical issues or questions
  • Share agricultural updates, tips, or notifications - Only if you opt in
  • Analyze website usage and performance - To enhance user experience
  • Ensure platform security - Protect against unauthorized access

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance - To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • Protection of Rights - To protect the rights, safety, and property of users and Healthy Harvest
  • Service Providers - With trusted partners who help operate our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, SMS services), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Aggregated Data - We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or statistical purposes

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure data storage with reputable cloud providers

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal requirements
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Object to processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Data portability - Request a copy of your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., weather services, market data sources). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when visiting these sites.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
