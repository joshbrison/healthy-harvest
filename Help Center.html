Frequently Asked Questions

What does Healthy Harvest do?

+

Healthy Harvest is an agricultural platform that helps farmers with:

  • AI-Powered Disease Detection: Upload photos of your plants to get instant diagnosis and treatment recommendations
  • Weather Forecasts: Access real-time and forecasted weather data tailored to your location
  • Market Prices: Check current crop prices and market trends
  • SMS Alerts: Receive important agricultural notifications on your phone
  • Agricultural Advice: Get personalized farming recommendations based on your crops and location

Is Healthy Harvest free to use?

+

Yes! Most features of Healthy Harvest are completely free for farmers:

  • Free Features: Disease diagnosis, weather forecasts, market prices, and basic alerts
  • Premium Features (Optional): Advanced analytics, historical data, and priority support
  • No Hidden Costs: We believe in supporting farmers, so core features will always remain free

If we introduce any paid features in the future, we will always offer a free alternative.

Do I need to create an account?

+

You can access some basic features without an account, but we recommend creating one for the best experience:

  • Without Account: View general information, access basic weather data
  • With Account: Save your farm location, track disease history, receive personalized alerts, save favorite crops
  • Account Benefits: Your data is saved across devices, you get personalized recommendations, and you can access your history anytime

Creating an account is quick, free, and requires only basic information.

How accurate is the disease diagnosis?

+

Our AI-based disease diagnosis has an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90% for common plant diseases:

  • Trained on Thousands of Images: Our model has been trained on a large dataset of plant disease images
  • Regular Updates: We continuously improve our AI with new data
  • Limitations: Accuracy depends on image quality, lighting, and plant type
  • Important: AI diagnosis is a tool to assist farmers, not replace professional agricultural experts

For critical farming decisions, always consult with local agricultural extension officers.

How do I receive SMS alerts?

+

To receive SMS alerts on your phone:

  1. Create an account and verify your email
  2. Go to your Profile Settings
  3. Add and verify your phone number
  4. Select the types of alerts you want to receive (weather warnings, price changes, disease alerts)
  5. Choose alert frequency (immediate, daily digest, or weekly summary)

Note: Standard SMS rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier. We don't charge for sending alerts, but your carrier might.

What crops and regions are supported?

+

Healthy Harvest currently supports:

  • Major Crops: Maize, rice, wheat, potatoes, tomatoes, beans, coffee, tea, and various vegetables
  • Regions: Available in most countries with focus on agricultural regions
  • Language: Currently available in English, with more languages coming soon
  • Expansion: We're continuously adding support for more crops and regions

If your crop or region isn't fully supported yet, you can still use many features like weather forecasts and market prices.
